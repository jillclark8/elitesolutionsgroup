ABOUT ELITE SOLUTIONS GROUP

Elite Solutions Group began as Elite Waterproofing, founded by Rick Crockett over 40 years ago with a commitment to protecting Colorado properties from water damage and deterioration.

In 2023, Mikey Sasina acquired the company with a bold vision: transform a specialized waterproofing contractor into a comprehensive commercial building solutions provider. Why juggle multiple contractors when one trusted team can handle it all?

In December 2025, we officially rebranded to Elite Solutions Group‚Äîreflecting our evolution beyond waterproofing into epoxy flooring, parking lot striping, snow removal, joint sealing, and more. Our technicians maintain industry certifications including OSHA safety training, manufacturer-specific installations, and specialized high-access work credentials.

Same expertise. Broader services. One trusted team serving Denver and greater Colorado, including mountain properties.

Whether you need emergency water damage response at 2 AM or a planned multi-phase renovation, Elite Solutions Group delivers precision application, quality craftsmanship, and coordinated teams on every project.

We serve Denver and greater Colorado, including mountain properties. Our teams are equipped to handle projects throughout the region with the same quality and reliability.

Yes, always. We provide free on-site inspections and detailed quotes for all services. No hidden fees, no surprises‚Äîjust honest pricing.

Most commercial epoxy flooring projects take 2-3 days from start to finish, depending on square footage and floor prep requirements. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your operations.
